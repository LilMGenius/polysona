# Polysona Mid Deck Design Notes (English)

## Visual Thesis
Use the README aurora SVG as the signature visual, keep the cold and precise agent-native mood, and let oversized typography plus proof-oriented copy communicate that this is not generic AI but a system for operating a person across multiple personas.

## Content Plan
- Opener: identity and name thesis
- Support / Proof: why current agents fail, and why the opportunity is urgent
- Detail / Story: interview -> structuring -> generation -> QA
- Close: Ralph operating capability and current traction
